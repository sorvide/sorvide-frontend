Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 23, 2025

1. Introduction

Sorvide AI ("Sorvide," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension and website (collectively, the "Service"). Please read this policy carefully. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Research Content: Text you manually highlight and submit for analysis through our Chrome extension
  • Webpage Metadata: Page title, URL, and author information (when available) for citation generation
  • License Information: License keys for Pro plan activation

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Feature usage, daily summary counts, and cooldown tracking
  • Technical Data: Browser type, extension version, and device identifier for license validation
  • History: No browsing history or personal information is collected automatically

2.3 Research Data

Important: For free users, text processing happens locally in your browser whenever possible. For Pro features requiring server processing, we use end-to-end encryption and do not permanently store your research content.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process text through AI models for summarization, plagiarism checking, insights, and citations
  • Validate Pro plan licenses and enforce usage limits
  • Store analysis history locally in your browser for your convenience
  • Improve our AI models and extension functionality (anonymized data only)

4. Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Local Storage: All user data (history, preferences, license) is stored locally in your browser
  • API Encryption: All data sent to our backend is encrypted in transit
  • No Permanent Storage:: Research text is processed and not stored permanently on our servers

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With vendors who help us operate the Service (e.g., Stripe for payments)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger or acquisition
  • With Your Consent: When you direct us to share information

6. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary:

  • Browser Storage: Data persists until you clear browser storage or uninstall the extension
  • Server Processing: Research text is processed temporarily and immediately discarded
  • License Information: Stored only for validation purposes

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have these rights:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your information
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@sorvide.com.

8. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the Service to function
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand usage patterns
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling essential cookies may affect Service functionality.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

10. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: privacy@sorvide.com
